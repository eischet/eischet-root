For creating new projects based on this template.

Public projects:

    <parent>
        <groupId>com.eischet.root</groupId>
        <artifactId>lib-public</artifactId>
        <version>1.0.3</version>
    </parent>

Internal projects:

    <parent>
        <groupId>com.eischet.root</groupId>
        <artifactId>lib-private</artifactId>
        <version>1.0.3</version>
    </parent>

For deploying artifacts to our internal repos:

    <build>
        <extensions>
            <extension>
                <groupId>com.eischet.mvn</groupId>
                <artifactId>wagon-providers</artifactId>
                <version>1.0.3</version>
            </extension>
        </extensions>
        ...
    </build>

For resolving the parent POM from our internal repos:

    <repositories>
        <repository>
            <id>eischet-public-releases</id>
            <url>https://mvn.eischet.net/public/releases</url>
            <snapshots><enabled>false</enabled></snapshots>
            <releases><enabled>true</enabled></releases>
        </repository>
        <repository>
            <id>eischet-public-snapshots</id>
            <url>https://mvn.eischet.net/public/snapshots</url>
            <snapshots><enabled>true</enabled></snapshots>
            <releases><enabled>false</enabled></releases>
        </repository>
    </repositories>

When setting up a new machine / build server, add files to ~/.m2/repository/.remoteRepositoryFilters named:

prefixes-eischet-internal-releases.txt
prefixes-eischet-internal-snapshots.txt
prefixes-eischet-public-releases.txt
prefixes-eischet-public-snapshots.txt

containing this text:

## repository-prefixes/2.0
com/eischet/
net/eischet/

This will save a lot of bandwidth, thank you.
