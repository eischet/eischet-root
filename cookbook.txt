For creating new projects based on this template.

Public projects:

    <parent>
        <groupId>com.eischet</groupId>
        <artifactId>eischet-root-public</artifactId>
        <version>1.0.2-SNAPSHOT</version>
    </parent>

Internal projects:

    <parent>
        <groupId>com.eischet</groupId>
        <artifactId>eischet-root-private</artifactId>
        <version>1.0.2-SNAPSHOT</version>
    </parent>

For deploying artifacts to our internal repos:

    <build>
        <extensions>
            <extension>
                <groupId>com.eischet.mvn</groupId>
                <artifactId>wagon-providers</artifactId>
                <version>1.0.2</version>
            </extension>
        </extensions>
        ...
    </build>
